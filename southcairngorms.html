Bod an Deamhain: April 2004

This must be perhaps the first time that I had walked in The Cairngorms and I've no recollection of using a bike so it was probably my first experience of the "Long Walk". Ultimately, the next trip, bikes were to replace these long walks into and out of the hills. On looking at the photos though it appears that I used my rigid Trek950.

Ben Macdhui: 2005

Such a cool walk this. I think I headed up to Linn of Dee straight after work when School broke up for the summer holidays. It was unfinished business to get up to the two peaks of Carn an T-sabhail and Sgorr an Lochain Uaine, after the miserable weather of the previous expedition with Callum. This was an overnight walk in midsummer, which made for fantastic views and pretty clear skies. Great conditions and amazing views north to Briaraech and East to Macdui.
